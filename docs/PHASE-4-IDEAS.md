# Phase 4 Ideas & Future Enhancements

## High Priority (Based on Industry Trends)

### Share of Model (SoM) Analytics
**Priority:** HIGH - Active initiative at work (using Profound platform)

**Features:**
- Brand visibility tracking across LLMs (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity)
- Simulate 100-500 prompts per brand/product category
- Competitive benchmarking (brand position vs competitors)
- Trend tracking over time (weekly/monthly)
- Integration with existing analytics dashboards

**Implementation:**
- New analytics endpoint: `GET /api/analytics/share-of-model`
- LLM query simulator (batch prompt testing)
- Competitive analysis engine
- Trend visualization (Share of Model over time)
- Alert system (SoM drops/rises)

**Business Value:**
- Measure brand presence in AI-mediated discovery
- Track competitive position in AI responses
- Optimize content for AI visibility
- Align with Feb 2026 industry trends (AI Marketing Conference)

### Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
**Priority:** HIGH - Emerging discipline replacing traditional SEO

**Features:**
- AI visibility audit for campaigns and landing pages
- Test how content appears in AI summaries
- Recommendations for AI-friendly copy structure
- Keyword optimization for AI responses
- Content scoring (AI discoverability score)
- A/B testing (AI-optimized vs standard copy)

**Implementation:**
- GEO audit tool (analyze campaign copy)
- AI summary simulator (how ads appear in AI responses)
- Content optimization recommendations
- GEO score calculator
- Integration with A/B testing framework

**Business Value:**
- Future-proof campaigns for AI discovery channels
- Optimize content for "zero-click" world
- Improve brand visibility in AI chat interfaces
- Competitive advantage in emerging channel
